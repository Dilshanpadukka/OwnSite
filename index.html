Welcome to the Sri Lanka Railway

Welcome to the official homepage of Sri Lanka Railway, your gateway to exploring the scenic beauty and rich heritage of Sri Lanka by train. Established in 1858, Sri Lanka Railway, also known as Sri Lanka Railways (SLR), has a storied history as one of the oldest and most charming railway networks in Asia. Our mission is to provide a safe, reliable, and comfortable rail service that connects the island’s diverse regions, facilitating travel for both locals and visitors alike.

A Journey Through Time

Sri Lanka Railway's history is deeply intertwined with the island’s colonial past and economic development. The first train journey, from Colombo to Ambepussa, marked the beginning of a network that would grow to cover over 1,500 kilometers of track, connecting major cities, towns, and rural areas. This extensive network includes the iconic Main Line, which travels through the lush tea plantations of the central highlands, and the Coastal Line, offering breathtaking views of the Indian Ocean.

Connecting Communities and Cultures

Sri Lanka Railway plays a crucial role in connecting the diverse communities and cultures across the island. Our trains are a lifeline for many, providing essential transportation for daily commuters, students, and businesses. Additionally, we support tourism by offering convenient access to some of Sri Lanka’s most popular destinations, including historic cities, national parks, and coastal resorts.

Our History The Beginnings

The Sri Lanka Railway System was established during the British colonial era, with the first train journey between Colombo and Ambepussa in 1864. This marked the start of a railway network that would become an integral part of Sri Lanka's transportation infrastructure.

Expansion and Growth

Over the decades, the railway network expanded to cover over 1,500 kilometers, connecting major cities, towns, and rural areas. Key lines include the Main Line, traversing the central highlands, and the Coastal Line, offering scenic views of the Indian Ocean.

Modernization

In recent years, the Sri Lanka Railway System has undergone significant modernization efforts. These include the introduction of new trains, upgrading of tracks, and implementation of advanced safety measures, all aimed at enhancing passenger experience and efficiency.

The Beauty of Sri Lanka by Train...

Scenic Routes

Traveling by train in Sri Lanka offers an unparalleled opportunity to witness the island’s natural beauty. The journey from Kandy to Ella is world-renowned for its stunning vistas of rolling hills, tea plantations, and mist-covered mountains. The Coastal Line from Colombo to Matara provides picturesque views of the coastline and beaches.

Natural Landscapes

From lush rainforests to serene beaches, the railway passes through diverse landscapes that highlight Sri Lanka's rich natural heritage. Enjoy the sight of cascading waterfalls, expansive paddy fields, and dense forests from the comfort of your train seat.

Cultural Heritage

Sri Lanka's railway network also connects passengers to numerous cultural and historical sites. Visit ancient cities like Anuradhapura and Polonnaruwa, explore the colonial architecture of Galle, and experience the vibrant culture of Jaffna.
